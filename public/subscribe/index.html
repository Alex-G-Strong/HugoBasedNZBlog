For RSS!#

If you dont know what RSS is, dont worry. Subscribe to the email and call it a day.

For those who want to dive in, RSS stands for “Really Simple Syndication”. It’s a way to get updates from a lot of websites without needing to visit them directly.

  1. Get an RSS Reader

Here are the most popular (and free) apps:

Phone App: NetNewsWire (simple and clean) or Feedly.
For your Browser (chrome, edge, safari, or firefox): Inoreader or Feedbro.

  1. Add My Feed Once you have an app, just click “Add New Source” and paste this link: https://www.alexstrong.design/index.xml

  2. Read at Your Own Pace Every time I push a new post from the road, it will automatically pop up in your app. No tracking, no ads, just the writing.
